Unlocking the Potential of Interactive Video

Traditionally, video resources have been underutilized in education. However, by making video content interactive, we can introduce complex yet engaging assessment mechanisms that truly verify a learner's understanding before they progress.

Our simple and intuitive editor tool allows course designers and instructors bring their video content to life without the need for Hollywood-style production facilities!

This innovative approach leads to deeper comprehension and improved retention of the material.

A Dynamic Learning Experience

Lending itself to BYOD strategies, tapybl is designed around smart devices, offering a comprehensive range of in-video interactive features, such as content branching, audio and video analysis, gradable videos and supplemental learning.

These features create a dynamic and engaging learning experience, ensuring that learners are actively involved and motivated throughout their educational journey.
